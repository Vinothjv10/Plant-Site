    Fig being a deciduous and sub tropical tree, prefers areas having arid or semiarid environment, high summer temperature, plenty of sunshine and moderate water.

    Quality figs are produced in the region with dry climate especially at the time of fruit development and maturity.

    Fruit Details

    Soil : Organically rich, moist, well-drained.
    Temperature:not drop below 15° F.
    Fertilizer :like formula 10-10-10 or 8-8-8
    Feature :Broad, rough, deciduous leaves

  • Outdoor: Full sun

  • Scientific Name: Ficus carica
  • Plant Type: Fruit tree
