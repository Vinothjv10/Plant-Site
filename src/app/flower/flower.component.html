Cupid Bows

Description

Cupid Bow plant (Achimenes longiflora) has many common names including Cupid's bow, nut-orchid, magic flower and hot water plant.This perennial tropical plant is typically grown as an indoor, container plant. It can also be grown as an annual in non-tropical environments.

It can grow up to 60 cm (24 in) long, arising from small rhizomes. The hairy leaves have saw toothed edges and can be up to 90 mm (3+1⁄2 in) long and 40 mm (1+1⁄2 in) wide. The flowers are produced from June to October and are usually blue with a white throat. They can be quite large – up to 50 mm (2 in) long and 75 mm (3 in) across.

Plant Details

Sunlight :3-4 hours per day (20-50% shade)
Soil :miracle-gro*cactus,palm&citrus potting mix,pH around 6-7.
Temperature:122-158F (50-70 degree C)
Fertilizer :Use a high-nitrogen fertilizer or top dress with alfalfa meal
Availability :Spring, Summer, Year Round

Key points

  • Outdoor: Direct sunlight
  • Height: 12 inches Approx
  • Watering: Once a day
  • Scientific Name: Achimenes longiflora
  • Life Cycle: Perennial

    • Care instruction

  • Water regularly. Do not let soil get dry.
  • Provide six hours of sunlight on a daily basis.
  • Add the fertiliser monthly to the soil.
  • Be careful to avoid overwatering and prune your plants if neccessary.
  • Remove faded flowers by pinching off at the base of the flower stem. This keeps the plant looking clean and healthy, and often stimulates the development of new flowers.

    Peace Lily Golden Touch

    Peace Lily Golden Touch

    Description

    Peace lilies are tropical species and hybrid from the Spathiphyllum genus that are a favorite flowering houseplant.Peace lilies are not true lilies (Lilium spp.) at all, but rather a member of the Araceae family. Its flowers resemble those of the calla lily (both plants belong to the same family) and is the reason for its name. The showy part of the flower features a white, hoodlike sheath (known as a spathe) which resembles a white flag of surrender.

    There are a wide variety of sizes and types of peace lilies. Most serve as floor plants since they can reach three feet tall and grow wide with big, bold leaves. Mauna loa supreme (a Spathiphyllum hybrid) is the standard midsize type. It grows up to four feet tall and features bronze-green foliage with an abundance of large, cupped spathes. It finally got the recognition it deserves from the general public after NASA put it on its list of “Top Ten Household Air Cleaning Plants.” This tropical shade-loving plant helps cleanse the air we breathe. While we all appreciate cleaner, oxygenated air, it’s also the easy peace lily care, resiliency and forgiving nature that makes them such popular houseplants.
